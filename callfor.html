CALL FOR PAPERS

The African Science Frontiers Initiatives Research Journal (ASFIRJ) is inviting submissions of papers to its maiden issue scheduled for publication on 15th September 2024.
ASFIRJ is the official journal of the African Science Frontiers Initiatives (ASFI). It is an international online-only open access multidisciplinary journal. ASFIRJ aims to be a leading research journal in Africa, with an unwavering emphasis on quality research and impact.
ASFIRJ accepts contributions from all countries of the world and publishes original papers, expert reviews, systematic reviews and meta-analyses, position papers, guidelines, protocols, data, editorials, news and commentaries, research letters from any research field.

Submission portal: Manuscripts must only be submitted via the submission portal here: https://cutt.ly/ReqH8Ght . .

Important Dates:

Paper Submission Deadline: 30th June 2024
Decision on Papers Sent Out for Peer Review: 21st July 2024
Accepted Papers Published Online: Latest 15th September 2024

Criterion:

All submitted manuscripts will undergo a thorough peer-review and will be judged and accepted solely on the basis of their scientific merit and quality.

Manuscript Formats:

Authors should carefully read the authorsâ€™ instructions and structure their manuscripts according to the stipulated formats and guidelines for each manuscript type. Link to authors' instructions: https://asfirj.org/authors.html .

Article Publication Fee (APF)

Although ASFIRJ charges modest APF for manuscripts submitted to it, all submissions under the current call (i.e. submitted latest 30th June 2024) will be published free of charge.

More information:
For more information about this call, please visit ASFIRJ website at: https://asfirj.org/. You can also write to: submissions@asfirj.org.
